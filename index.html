Hello.

Liam McColgan is a Peterborough based Designer who loves to help people connect their business and passions through the power of the internet.

He uses his ever expanding knowledge of HTML, CSS and Javascript to help his clients present their own vision and footprint on the web.

Building websites for nearly a year, Liam has built upon core skills in Web Design; creating a foundation in which to explore and nurture the endless possibilities of an ever evolving industry.

He believes in a life where work and play are forever intertwined.

To see and reach full potential, you must first have the passion, knowledge and confidence to realise and execute your ideas.

As of 2018, he is designing and creating websites for local people and businesses who want a thoughtful, bespoke, user friendly site to gain web presence and ultimately customer turnover.
