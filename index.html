Hello.

I'm a UK based Designer helping people reach their business and passions to others through the power of the internet.

Using my ever expanding knowledge of HTML, CSS and Javascript, I enable my clients to present their own vision and footprint on the web.

Building websites for nearly a year, Liam has built upon core skills in Web Design; creating a foundation in which to explore and nurture the endless possibilities of an ever evolving industry.

I pursue a life where work and play are forever intertwined. Blurring the lines of work and passion ultimately pushes you to become the best you can at what you do.

To see and reach full potential, you must first have the passion, knowledge and confidence to realise and execute your ideas.

As of 2018, I'm designing and creating websites for local people and businesses who want a thoughtful, bespoke, user friendly site to gain web presence and ultimately customer turnover.
